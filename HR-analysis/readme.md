## Objective
- Analyse key factors affecting employee retention and workforce stability. 
- Recommend strategies to improve retention and optimize staffing.

## Tools used
- Power BI

## Dataset
Source - Kaggle HR dataset

## Process 
- Data transformation and cleaning
- measures creation
- Dashboard creation
- Insight analysis

## Key Observations 
- involuntary termination was the major reason of attrition in the company
- full-time staffs had a longer ternure than contract or part-time staffs
- high performing staffs stayed longer in the company than lower perfoming staffs

## Recommendations
- focus recuitment of full-time staffs
- reward the high perfomers in the company
- Train staffs to better their performance
