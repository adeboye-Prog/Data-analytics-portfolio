Sales Performance Analysis

## Objective
- Analyze spending patterns by gender,
- Track most popular categories over time,
- Predict total amount spent based on customer features,
- Identify seasonal or monthly spikes in certain categories.

## Tools used
- Excel
- Power Bi

## Dataset
Source : Kaggle Sales dataset

## Process
1. Data cleaning
2. Pivot Analysis
3. Dashboard Creation
4. Insight generation

## Observations
- Female consumers purchase more products than males
- clothing was the most sold product over time
- the youngest age-group category (18-28) spent the most money

## Recommendations
- increase marketing budget on electronics and beauty products
- focus on retaining more customers in the (18-28) age group, as they purchase the most items.
- there should be an increase in inventory for clothing,as it is in high demand.
